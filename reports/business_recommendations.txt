BUSINESS RECOMMENDATIONS

1) Tighten approvals for high-risk segments
- Add stricter underwriting checks where loan_to_income_ratio is high.
- Require extra verification for weaker credit profiles.

2) Use risk-based pricing
- Tie pricing tiers to predicted default probability.
- Limit exposure for high-risk purpose categories.

3) Improve monitoring cadence
- Track monthly default rate and high-risk population share.
- Trigger portfolio review if default rate breaches threshold.

4) Prioritize collections by risk score
- Focus collections outreach on high-risk active accounts.
- Create preventive campaigns for medium-risk segments.

5) Establish model governance
- Re-train quarterly.
- Monitor AUC and calibration by risk band.
